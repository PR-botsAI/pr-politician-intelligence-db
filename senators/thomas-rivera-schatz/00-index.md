## 3-Sentence Summary
Thomas Rivera Schatz is Puerto Rico's Senate President and one of its most powerful and controversial political figures — a PNP hardliner known for his iron discipline, aggressive statehood advocacy, and frequent conflicts with governors of his own party. An attorney by training with a law degree from Rutgers, he has been one of the most significant legislative forces in modern PR history. His high profile and massive digital presence make him a premium target, but his closeness to PNP power means he likely already has staff handling digital — pitch the upgrade.
