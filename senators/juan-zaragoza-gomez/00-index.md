## 3-Sentence Summary
Juan Zaragoza Gómez is one of Puerto Rico's most technically sophisticated legislators, bringing his experience as former Treasury Secretary and economist to the Senate minority caucus. As a PPD senator at-large, he focuses on fiscal policy, federal funding, and budget analysis — the kind of detailed, technical advocacy that rarely makes headlines but shapes policy. His technocratic credibility makes him a different kind of PPD voice in the Senate.

## Background Note
Zaragoza served as Secretary of Treasury under Governor García Padilla (2014–2017) — the administration that declared PR's debt unpayable and led to PROMESA. This is both a credential (he handled the most difficult fiscal crisis in PR history) and a political vulnerability (the debt crisis unfolded on his watch).
