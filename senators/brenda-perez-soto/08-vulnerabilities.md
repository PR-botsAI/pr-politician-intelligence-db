# Political Vulnerabilities — Brenda Pérez Soto

> Internal document. Use to craft relevant pitch angles.

## Electoral Vulnerabilities

**Mountain interior PPD strength:**
- Lares and Utuado have strong PPD historical traditions
- In a non-wave year, these municipalities could flip back to PPD
- She won in 2024 on PNP wave energy; 2028 will test her personal base
- She needs to build personal loyalty in interior municipalities beyond party loyalty

**Geographic scale:**
- Serving 7 municipalities spread from coast to mountains is physically difficult
- Constituents in Lares and Utuado may feel less served than those in Arecibo
- Requires significant travel and logistical investment to maintain presence everywhere

**Freshmen senator status:**
- Limited legislative track record (15 months)
- Still building relationships within the Senate hierarchy
- Not yet chairing committees (requires seniority and Rivera Schatz's favor)
