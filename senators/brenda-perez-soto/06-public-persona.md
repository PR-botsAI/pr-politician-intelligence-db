# Public Persona — Brenda Pérez Soto

## Brand Summary

Brenda Pérez Soto presents as a community-service-first senator — accessible, active in the district, focused on practical results. Her public persona is less ideological and more practical than many legislators. She goes where her constituents are: service fairs in Lares, constituent events in Quebradillas, advocacy on rural issues like horse road safety.
