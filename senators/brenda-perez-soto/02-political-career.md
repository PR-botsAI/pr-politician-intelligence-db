## The Senatorial District III Context

Distrito Senatorial III elects 2 senators. Pérez Soto holds one of those seats. The second senator should be researched to understand the full legislative representation of the Arecibo region.

**Why this district is complex to serve:**
- 7+ municipalities covering coastal AND mountain interior
- Different economic profiles: coastal commerce (Arecibo, Hatillo) vs. mountain agriculture (Lares, Utuado)
- Different infrastructure needs: PRASA Northern Region, mountain roads, coastal flooding
- Historical PPD strength in mountain municipalities vs. PNP coastal strength
