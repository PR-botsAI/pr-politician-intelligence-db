# Belief System — Brenda Pérez Soto

## Core Political Identity
Brenda Pérez Soto is a mainstream PNP senator with a community-service-first orientation. Her public legislative activity centers on practical quality-of-life issues for her largely rural/semi-rural district rather than grand ideological battles. She is a pragmatic institutionalist.

## Issue Positions

### Comunidad y Servicios (Community & Services) — PRIMARY
- Organizes "Tu Distrito en Acción" style service fairs
- Brings government agencies directly to constituents
- Focus on practical, tangible constituent services

### Seguridad Vial y Animales (Road Safety)
- **Documented:** PS 1159 — mandatory microchip registration for horses to address road safety
- Practical rural issue: stray horses on PR roads cause accidents, particularly in interior municipalities
- Shows sensitivity to agricultural community (Hatillo dairy, horse culture in interior PR)

### Infraestructura Rural
- Serves municipalities spread across the Norte and into mountain interior
- Road connectivity between Arecibo coast and Lares/interior is a key district need
- Water and utility service to rural sectors
