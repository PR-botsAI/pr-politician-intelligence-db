## 3-Sentence Summary
Brenda Pérez Soto is the PNP Senator representing Senatorial District III (Arecibo), appointed by Senate President Thomas Rivera Schatz to chair the powerful Comisión de Educación, Arte y Cultura. A former teacher, school counselor, and school principal who worked in the Arecibo Regional Education Office, she has a deep personal and professional connection to the education system she now legislates over. She has authored landmark legislation — including a unanimously-approved AI education bill and a horse registration bill — while also running community service events across her large 8-municipality district.

## Documented Legislative Achievements
1. **PS 348 (Approved by Senate unanimously, June 2025)** — "Ley de Inteligencia Artificial como herramienta de trabajo e instruccional para estudiantes y maestros en el Departamento de Educación de PR" — AI integration in public schools for students with academic gaps and special education
2. **PS 374 (Approved)** — "Ley para el Destaque de Empleados Públicos como Asistentes de Servicio (T1) de sus Hijos en el Programa de Educación Especial" — allows public employees to serve as T1 assistants for their special ed children
3. **PS 820 (Approved)** — "Ley de Alfabetización Mediática e Informacional de Puerto Rico" — media/digital literacy including responsible AI use in public school curriculum
4. **PS 1159 (Filed)** — Mandatory microchip registration for horses on PR roads (stray horse road safety crisis)
5. **Programa de Patrulleros Escolares** (Nov 2025) — Reactivated Education-Police partnership forming student leaders across Arecibo district schools
