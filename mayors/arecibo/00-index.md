## 3-Sentence Summary
Carlos "Tito" Ramírez is Arecibo's PPD mayor, re-elected in November 2024 to a second term after defeating the PNP challenger by roughly 2,000 votes in a competitive race. He came to office in 2020 by defeating the PNP incumbent Carlos Molina, ending a period of PNP control, and has governed during the PRASA Norte crisis and ongoing Hurricane Maria recovery. As the mayor of our home municipality and the Norte region hub, he is both a natural first client and our strongest local credibility anchor.
