## 3-Sentence Summary
Carlos "Carlitos" Román Román became Hatillo’s mayor in May 2022 via a PPD special election after longtime mayor José "Chely" Rodríguez resigned for health reasons, winning that internal PPD election with 87% of the vote. He then won the 2024 general election outright with 53% against four opponents from PNP, PD, and PIP. He governs Puerto Rico’s dairy capital with 33 years of public service background.
