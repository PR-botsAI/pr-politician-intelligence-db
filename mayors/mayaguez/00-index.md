## Key Facts
- Puerto Rico's third or fourth largest city (depends on census year)
- University hub: Universidad de Puerto Rico Mayagüez (RUM) — engineering school
- Port city with ferry service to Dominican Republic
- Western regional commercial center
- 2020 earthquake zone — some impacts

## Key Municipal Issues
1. University-town dynamics — student population + local residents
2. Port development
3. Economic development in western PR (historically underserved vs. metro)
4. Earthquake recovery

## Pitch Notes
- **University angle:** RUM students are digital-native constituents; mayor needs to communicate in their channels
- **Western PR market:** Less competition for us; good initial market outside norte
