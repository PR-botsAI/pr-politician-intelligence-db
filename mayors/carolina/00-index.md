## Key Facts
- Carolina is home to Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport — PR's main airport
- Major tourism gateway
- Isla Verde beach resort area — high-end tourism zone
- Roberto Clemente's hometown — strong cultural pride
- Industrial and commercial development east of San Juan

## Key Municipal Issues
1. Airport corridor development and traffic
2. Tourism infrastructure and hospitality industry
3. Flood zone management (Isla Verde flood risks)
4. Public safety in urban zones
