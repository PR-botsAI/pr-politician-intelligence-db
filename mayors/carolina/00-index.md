## 3-Sentence Summary
José Carlos Aponte Dalmau won re-election as Carolina’s PPD mayor in 2024 with 26,873 votes, comfortably defeating PNP’s Humberto Cobo Estrella (10,867 votes). He governs Puerto Rico’s third largest municipality by population, which includes Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport — the island’s main airport — and the Isla Verde hotel and resort corridor. His high constituent volume and diverse tourism economy make him a premium digital services target.
