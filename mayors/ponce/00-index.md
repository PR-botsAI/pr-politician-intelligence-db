## Why Ponce Matters
Ponce is Puerto Rico's historic second city and the cultural capital of the south. The Ponce mayor is one of the most visible municipal leaders in PR. A demo page for Ponce would demonstrate our capability to serve major cities.

## Ponce Profile

### History & Character
- Founded 1692; named after Ponce de León's great-grandson
- Known for distinct Ponce identity and pride (Ponceños are famously proud of their city)
- Architecture: stunning neoclassical buildings, the famous black-and-red firehouse (Parque de Bombas)
- Cultural institutions: Museo de Arte de Ponce (world-class collection), Museo de la Historia de Ponce
- The city has suffered significant population decline and economic challenges

### Economy
- Historically industrial: Ponce was a major manufacturing center
- Post-Maria: severe economic challenges
- Port: Ponce has PR's major southern deep-water port
- Pharmaceutical manufacturing in the region
- Commercial center for the south

### Infrastructure
- Post-2020 earthquake: Some damage in surrounding southwestern municipalities
- Energy: Southern grid separate challenges
- Water: Different PRASA region than Norte

### Political Profile
- Luis A. Ferré, PNP founder, was from Ponce — gives Ponce special PNP historical significance
- Has elected both PNP and PPD mayors
- Research 2024 result

## Top Issues for Ponce Mayor
1. Economic revitalization — reversing population decline and economic decay
2. Heritage preservation — protecting Ponce's architectural and cultural treasures
3. Port development — maximizing the southern port's economic potential
4. Post-earthquake recovery in the broader region
5. Energy (south PR has different grid challenges)
6. Tourism development around Ponce's cultural assets
