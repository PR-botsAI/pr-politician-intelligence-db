## 3-Sentence Summary
Marlese Sifre Toro won the 2024 mayoral election in Ponce, Puerto Rico’s second largest city, becoming the first PPD mayor of Ponce in years and defeating PNP challenger Pablo Colón Santiago with 29,799 votes. She governs a culturally rich city anchored by the Museo de Arte de Ponce, Carnaval de Ponce, and a significant post-earthquake recovery effort following the 2020 earthquakes. As a newly elected female mayor of the island’s second city, her brand-building needs are high and her window for digital infrastructure setup is now.
