## Key Facts
- Puerto Rico's second largest city
- Cultural center — Museo de Arte de Ponce (world-class), Carnaval de Ponce
- Founded by Luis A. Ferré's family — strong PNP historical connection
- Hit by 2020 earthquakes — recovery ongoing
- Growing tourism sector

## Key Municipal Issues
1. Earthquake recovery — 2020 earthquakes left many structures damaged
2. Economic development — port, industry, tourism
3. Education — Ponce has major educational institutions (Pontifical Catholic, Inter)
4. Healthcare — regional medical center
5. Cultural heritage preservation
