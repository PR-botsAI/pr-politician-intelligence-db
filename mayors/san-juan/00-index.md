## Why San Juan Matters
San Juan is the capital, the most visible municipality in Puerto Rico, and the most watched mayoral position. The San Juan mayor is a national figure in Puerto Rican politics. A San Juan hype page demo would be the most impressive showcase in our portfolio.

## San Juan Profile
- **Capital city:** Government center; Fortaleza (Governor), Capitol (Legislature), federal courts
- **Tourism:** Old San Juan, Condado, Isla Verde — major tourism economy
- **Culture:** Santurce arts district, Miramar, Ocean Park
- **Business:** Major commerce, banking, professional services hub
- **Neighborhoods:** Old San Juan, Condado, Miramar, Santurce, Río Piedras, Hato Rey (financial district)

## Political History
- Carmen Yulin Cruz (PPD) was mayor 2013–2020 — became internationally known during Hurricane Maria for feuding with Trump on Twitter
- She chose not to run for re-election in 2020
- Research: Who won 2020 and 2024 mayoral race?

## Top Issues for San Juan Mayor
1. Tourism development and management
2. Gentrification / displacement (Act 60 investor influx into Santurce/Ocean Park)
3. Infrastructure: flooding (San Juan floods severely), roads
4. Homelessness (highest concentration in PR)
5. Economic development: keeping businesses in the city
6. Quality of life for residents being priced out
