## 3-Sentence Summary
Miguel Romero Lugo won re-election as San Juan’s PNP mayor in November 2024 with approximately 46% of the vote, defeating MVC’s Manuel Natal Albelo (who ran a strong second), PPD’s Terestella González Denton, and others in a fragmented field. He governs Puerto Rico’s capital city and largest municipality with a population of ~318,000, managing everything from Viejo San Juan’s historic district to Santurce’s arts scene to Rio Piedras’ university community. The capital city mayor commands the largest budget, the highest media profile, and the most complex constituent communications needs of any Puerto Rico municipality.
