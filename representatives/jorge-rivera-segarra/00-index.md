## 3-Sentence Summary
Jorge Rivera Segarra represents Distrito 15, one of Puerto Rico's most geographically and historically significant interior mountain districts — covering Lares (birthplace of the Grito de Lares independence revolt of 1868), Utuado, Adjuntas, and Jayuya. These municipalities are deeply rural, mountainous, and still recovering from Hurricane Maria (2017) and the 2020 earthquakes. A PNP representative in historically PPD-leaning mountain country, he must serve conservative constituents while delivering for communities with complex political histories.

## District Character
- **Interior mountain district** — most different from our primary Arecibo/Hatillo target
- **Lares:** Historically significant (Grito de Lares 1868); strong PPD/independence tradition; coffee country
- **Utuado:** Mountain municipality; major Hurricane Maria damage; Dos Bocas reservoir (PRASA source)
- **Adjuntas:** Coffee country; environmental advocacy tradition (Casa Pueblo); PPD traditional base
- **Jayuya:** Home of the Taino Museum; 1950 Nationalist Revolt site; coffee and farming

## Top Issues for District 15
1. Hurricane Maria recovery — still incomplete in 2026
2. Road infrastructure — mountain roads critically damaged
3. Agricultural support — coffee industry revitalization
4. Water — Dos Bocas reservoir feeds the Norte region
5. Economic development — persistent poverty in mountain interior
