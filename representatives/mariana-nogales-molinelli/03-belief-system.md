# Belief System — Mariana Nogales Molinelli

## Core Political Identity
Mariana Nogales is Puerto Rico's clearest progressive-left legislator — a lawyer by training who applies a rights-based, decolonization framework to every political issue. She is explicitly feminist, explicitly LGBTQ+-affirming, and explicitly anti-colonial. Her political identity is ideologically coherent and consistently expressed.

## Status Position
- **Affiliated with PIP** (Partido Independentista Puertorriqueño) and MVC alliance
- Operates within a decolonization framework
- Does not frame status in electoral terms as her primary pitch — frames it as a justice issue
- Would not support statehood; views it as continuation of colonial dynamic

## Issue Positions (Documented)

### LGBTQ+ Rights — SIGNATURE ISSUE
- One of the most vocal LGBTQ+ allies in the PR legislature
- Advocates for: marriage equality enforcement, trans rights, anti-discrimination protections
- Has challenged colleagues on homophobic statements or votes
- Her LGBTQ+ advocacy is a core part of her political brand and base

### Vivienda / Housing
- Strongly opposes Act 60 (formerly Act 20/22) investor incentives as displacement engine
- Advocates for rent control, tenant protections
- Frames housing as a human right
- Has documented cases of Puerto Rican families displaced by wealthy investors

### Economía
- Anti-austerity, anti-PROMESA Fiscal Board
- Advocates for cancellation or audit of public debt
- Opposes privatization of public services (LUMA, etc.)
- Pro-worker, pro-union
- Pro-cooperative economy

### Corrupción y Transparencia
- One of the most consistent anti-corruption voices in the legislature
- Has demanded investigations into government contracts, spending
- Advocates for open government, public records access
- Built reputation by actually reading legislation and calling out problematic provisions publicly

### Género y Feminismo
- Explicit feminist framework
- Advocates for gender pay equity, protection from gender-based violence
- Tracks femicide data; has demanded emergency response
- Her feminism is intersectional — connects gender, race, class, colonialism

### Medio Ambiente
- Pro-renewable energy, anti-petrochem expansion
- Connects environmental destruction with colonial exploitation
- Advocates for 100% clean energy transition

## Rhetorical Patterns

- **Tone:** Passionate but controlled — not performative anger, genuine conviction
- **Hashtags:** Strategic use of hashtag campaigns
- **Language:** Spanish primary, bilingual when addressing U.S. audience

- She livestreams legislative proceedings with commentary
- She holds her coalition accountable publicly (not just the opposition)
- She has a genuine organic following — not bot-inflated
