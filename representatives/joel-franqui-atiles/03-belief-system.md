# Belief System — Joel Franqui Atiles

His politics are those of the PPD's pragmatic center: moderate economics, ELA status position (without intensity), local service focus.

## Issue Positions (Inferred from District)

### Agricultural Support — KEY ISSUE
- Hatillo's dairy industry depends on government policy (pricing, subsidies, quality standards)
- Camuy's agricultural sector needs support
- PPD tradition of supporting agricultural communities aligns with his district's needs
- Expected to advocate for: USDA programs, local agricultural incentives, dairy cooperative support

### Rural Community Services
- Camuy and Quebradillas are smaller municipalities with limited resources
- PPD's municipal machine (even though these municipalities may be split PNP/PPD) serves these communities
- Constituent service: ferias de servicios, bringing agencies to rural communities

### Norte Region Water
- Same PRASA Northern Region issues as Robles' district
- But PPD frames it differently: as PNP governance failure (PNP controls the government)
- Opportunity for PPD: "Under us, the AAA was better managed"

### Tourism
- Camuy Caves (Parque de las Cavernas del Río Camuy) is one of PR's great natural attractions
- PPD traditionally supports cultural tourism and natural heritage
