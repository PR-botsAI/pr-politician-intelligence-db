# Belief System — Joel Franqui Atiles

## Core Political Identity
Joel Franqui is a mainstream PPD legislator serving a largely rural, agricultural district. His political identity is community-service-first, with PPD's cultural nationalism as the ideological backdrop. As a minority party legislator, he has less legislative power than his PNP counterparts and must focus on visibility, community service, and constituent advocacy.

## Issue Positions

### Agricultura
- Hatillo is PR's dairy capital — agricultural support is a core district issue
- Expected position: Pro-agricultural subsidies, cooperative support
- PPD historically: pro-agricultural sector, defender of local food production

### Servicios Comunitarios
- Camuy, Quebradillas, and rural Hatillo sectors need rural community services
- Elderly care, roads, water — standard Norte rural needs

### Oposición al PNP
- In minority, his legislative role is partly oppositional — flag problems with majority bills
- PPD minority voice on consumer issues, privatization opposition
