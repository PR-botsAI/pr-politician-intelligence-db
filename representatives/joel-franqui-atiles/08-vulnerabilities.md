# Political Vulnerabilities — Joel Franqui Atiles

> Internal document for pitch intelligence.

## Electoral Vulnerabilities

**PNP Hatillo penetration:**
- Hatillo is a shared municipality with Robles (PNP, Dist. 14)
- As Robles builds his profile and delivers constituent service, Hatillo voters in Dist. 19 will compare
- If Robles is visibly effective and Franqui less so, Hatillo votes could shift in 2028

**Minority party disadvantages:**
- Cannot deliver government programs as easily as PNP majority
- PRASA, roads, government contracts — PNP controls distribution
- Constituents may blame him for problems he can't fix because his party doesn't control government

**Brand visibility:**
- As a PPD freshman in a minority party, he gets less media coverage than PNP representatives
- He must work harder to be visible without government machinery behind him

## Digital Vulnerabilities

- Almost certainly has no professional campaign/legislative website
- Limited digital reach compared to his PNP counterparts who have government resources
- Lead capture: zero
- Voice assistant: zero
- Constituent database: minimal

**This is the opportunity pitch:**
> "Representante Franqui, el PNP controla el gobierno. Tienen los recursos del Estado detrás de ellos. Usted compite con sus propias manos. Una plataforma digital profesional multiplica su alcance sin depender del partido en el poder."

## Our Value Proposition

- Level the playing field against PNP's resource advantage
- Build a constituent database NOW that becomes his 2028 campaign foundation
- Prove constituent service impact with data ("12,000 constituents reached this year")
- Make him as visible digitally as any PNP legislator with government websites
