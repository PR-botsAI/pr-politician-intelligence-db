# Public Persona — Joel Franqui Atiles

## Brand Summary
Joel Franqui is a community-focused PPD representative whose identity is rooted in the agricultural and coastal character of Hatillo, Camuy, and Quebradillas. As a minority legislator, his political effectiveness depends on high constituent visibility and local credibility rather than legislative power. His brand should be: "the man who fights for the Norte" from the PPD perspective.

## Communication Style
- **Tone:** Community-oriented, accessible, constituent-first
- **Language:** Spanish-dominant
- **Platform:** Facebook primary (typical for PPD Norte-region legislators)
- **Content type:** Community events, agricultural advocacy, coastal issues, PPD messaging on LUMA/PRASA failures

## Key Pitch Insight

Franqui is in a particularly interesting position for our pitch because:
- He is in the **minority** — no government resources to deliver services like PNP reps can
- He must **prove his value** to constituents through direct service and communication
- A professional digital platform **levels the playing field** against Robles (PNP) who has government backing
- Frame: "Como legislador del PPD, usted tiene que trabajar más duro para ser visto. Una plataforma digital profesional le pone en el mismo nivel que cualquier representante del partido mayoritario."
