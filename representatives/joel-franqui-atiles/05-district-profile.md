# District Profile — Distrito 19 (Hatillo, Camuy, Quebradillas)

> See also: `_DISTRICTS/representative-districts/distrito-19.md` for full profile.

## Geographic Overview
- **District:** Representativo 19
- **Municipalities:** Hatillo + Camuy + Quebradillas
- **Region:** Norte (northern coast) — west of Arecibo
- **Coast:** All three municipalities face the Atlantic Ocean (northern PR coast)

## Municipality Profiles

### Hatillo (~40,000)
- **Identity:** La Ciudad de las Máscaras; Puerto Rico's dairy capital
- **Economy:** Dairy cooperatives; commercial corridor on PR-2; some light industry
- **Cultural anchor:** Festival de Máscaras (December) — island-wide draw
- **Political:** Competitive PNP/PPD; agricultural communities have PPD tradition
- **Note:** Hatillo is ALSO in Robles' (PNP) Distrito 14 — Franqui (PPD) and Robles both serve Hatillo from different chambers

### Camuy (~33,000)
- **Identity:** Home of the Camuy Caves (Parque de las Cavernas del Río Camuy)
- **Camuy Caves:** One of the world's largest cave systems; major tourism asset for northern PR
- **Economy:** Some agriculture; tourism; coastal fishing
- **Political:** PPD-leaning (explains why Franqui held the seat despite PNP wave)
- **Cultural:** Río Camuy (Camuy River) runs through underground cave system — natural heritage

### Quebradillas (~25,000)
- **Identity:** Small coastal municipality; historic muelle (pier/dock)
- **Economy:** Fishing, some agriculture, coastal services
- **Tourism potential:** Muelle de Quebradillas area; coastal scenery
- **Political:** PPD-leaning (traditional fishing/agricultural community)
- **Character:** Quiet, small-town, coastal Puerto Rico — slower pace than Arecibo

## Top Issues for Distrito 19
1. **Dairy industry support** — Hatillo's entire economic identity; cooperative health is existential
2. **Camuy Caves development** — underutilized tourism gem; needs investment and promotion
3. **PRASA Norte** — same water crisis as Distrito 14; Camuy and Quebradillas also affected
4. **Coastal fishing support** — Quebradillas' traditional fishing community needs policy backing
5. **Agriculture** — Beyond dairy: Camuy and Quebradillas have diversified agriculture
6. **Road infrastructure** — Connecting these municipalities to each other and to Arecibo hub

## Political Profile
- **Competitive profile:** Distrito 19 is PPD-leaning but not safe; Franqui held it against the wave
- **Why PPD holds it:** Agricultural/rural communities with PPD historical roots in Camuy/Quebradillas
- **Why PNP competes:** Hatillo is more competitive; commercial growth attracts PNP voters
- **2028:** If Franqui delivers constituent service and the PPD recovers statewide, this is a PPD hold
