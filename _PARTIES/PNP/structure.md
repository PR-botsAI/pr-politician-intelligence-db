## How PNP Internal Politics Work

### The Governor-Senate Tension
Historically the most significant internal dynamic in PNP is the relationship between the Governor and the Senate President. Rivera Schatz and previous governors have clashed repeatedly. His Senate is semi-independent power center.

### The Primary System
- PNP holds primaries for all major offices
- Primaries can be vicious — PNP members attack each other more viciously than they attack the PPD
- Primary victories are the key competition; general elections in PNP strongholds are secondary
- The 2024 governor primary was between González Colón and Pedro Pierluisi — she won

### Legislative Discipline
- PNP legislators generally vote with the party on status-related legislation
- On local/district issues, individual legislators have more freedom
- The supermajority gives leadership enormous leverage over members
- New legislators (like Robles) typically follow leadership closely in first term
