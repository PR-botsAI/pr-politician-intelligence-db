# PIP — Voter Base Profile

## Who Votes PIP/Alianza

### Demographics
- **Age:** Bimodal — older committed independentistas (60+) and young progressive (18–35)
- **Geography:** Urban, university communities, cultural/arts communities
- **Education:** Highest education level base in PR
- **Occupation:** Professors, lawyers, artists, writers, students, community organizers
- **Religion:** Secular, progressive Catholic, or non-religious

### Psychographics
- **Identity:** Principled nationalist — Puerto Rican identity is primary and non-negotiable
- **Relationship to U.S.:** Complex — often appreciates individual Americans but deeply critical of U.S. government and colonial history
- **Values:** Justice, self-determination, dignity, intellectual honesty
- **Political engagement:** Very high — these are not passive voters
- **Historical consciousness:** Deeply aware of the carpetas, COINTELPRO, Albizu Campos, colonial history

### The PIP Voter Commitment
PIP voters vote with their conscience against electoral logic. They have voted for independence candidates when it was electorally futile for decades. This conviction makes them extremely loyal — a PIP voter who converts to our client is a committed advocate.

## What PIP Voters Want
1. Decolonization — genuine self-determination process
2. Cultural and linguistic sovereignty
3. Economic justice (anti-austerity, anti-privatization)
4. Environmental protection
5. Civil rights for all communities
6. Honest, clean governance
