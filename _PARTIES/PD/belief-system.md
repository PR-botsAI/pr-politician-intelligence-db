# Proyecto Dignidad — Belief System & Ideology

## Core Identity
Proyecto Dignidad is Puerto Rico's explicitly Christian-values party. It combines social conservatism (anti-abortion, traditional marriage, family values) with anti-corruption reform politics and a pragmatic status position (not a priority). It is the spiritual home of PR's evangelical political movement.

---

## Status Position
- **Official position:** Political status is NOT a priority
- Focuses on governance quality, ethics, family policy regardless of status
- Will work within whatever status framework Puerto Rico has
- This "status agnosticism" is strategic — allows voters from PNP/PPD/MVC backgrounds to join
- **Rhetorical frame:** "Lo que importa es cómo gobernamos, no bajo qué bandera"

---

## Social Ideology — PRIMARY IDENTITY

### Family & Marriage
- Traditional family definition (man + woman)
- Opposes same-sex marriage (challenges it legislatively and legally)
- Advocates for family-strengthening programs
- "La familia es la base de la sociedad" is a core belief

### Abortion
- Pro-life position
- Advocates for restrictions on abortion access
- Frames as protecting "la dignidad de la vida humana"

### Education
- Supports school choice, including religious schools
- Christian values in education
- Parental rights in education decisions

### LGBTQ+ Rights
- Opposes LGBTQ+ rights expansion
- Frames opposition in religious freedom terms
- Direct contrast to MVC's position

---

## Governance & Anti-Corruption
- Strong anti-corruption platform — distinguishes itself from PNP/PPD machine politics
- Advocates for government transparency
- Opposes patronage/clientelism
- This is what allows non-evangelical conservatives to join the party

---

## Economic Ideology
- Generally conservative-leaning
- Supports small business, local economy
- Less defined than social positions — governance reform is the economic focus
- Opposes government waste and corruption in spending

---

## Rhetorical Patterns
- **Key words:** "Dignidad" · "Familia" · "Fe" · "Valores" · "Honestidad" · "Transparencia"
- **Emotional frame:** Moral restoration of Puerto Rico; return to foundational values
- **Tone:** Preacher-adjacent — passionate, moral, community-rooted
- **Base communication channels:** Churches, WhatsApp groups, evangelical media, YouTube
