## Geographic Overview
The northwest corner of Puerto Rico. Aguadilla is a military/former military base area (former Ramey Air Force Base — now Rafael Hernández Airport). Agricultural, rural, with coastal communities popular for surfing (Rincón) and fishing. One of the most culturally distinct regions of PR.

## Key Facts
- **Rincón:** World-famous surfing destination; growing expat/digital nomad community; gentrification concerns
- **Rafael Hernández Airport:** Second-busiest airport in PR; major regional asset
- **Agriculture:** Coffee, plantains, tropical fruits
- **Military history:** Ramey base era created distinct community identity

## Key Issues
1. Agricultural support
2. Expat/investor displacement concerns (Rincón area Act 60 effects)
3. Airport-based economic development
4. Rural infrastructure
5. Hurricane Maria recovery (northwest hit hard)

## Political Significance
- Competitive district where both PNP and PPD can win
- MVC growing presence in Rincón progressive community
- Agricultural voters lean PPD; military-adjacent voters lean PNP
