# Senatorial District IV — Aguadilla / Mayagüez

## Overview
- **District Number:** IV (Cuarto)
- **Region:** Western Puerto Rico
- **Character:** Western coast and interior; agricultural, tourism, industrial; more economically challenged than metro

---

## Geographic Coverage
Distrito IV covers western PR, likely including:
- **Mayagüez:** ~73,000; second-largest city in PR (outside metro); university city (UPR Mayagüez — largest engineering school)
- **Aguadilla:** ~55,000; former U.S. Air Force base (now Rafael Hernández Airport, international flights); tourist destination (Crash Boat Beach)
- **Cabo Rojo:** ~50,000; southwestern coast; tourist beaches, salt flats, lighthouse
- **Ríncon:** ~14,000; famous surf destination; expat community
- **San Sebastián:** ~40,000; interior, coffee, PD's founder Javier Jiménez was mayor here
- **Aguada, Añasco, Hormigueros, Moca, Isabela** (various smaller western municipalities)

## Character

- **UPR Mayagüez (RUM):** Puerto Rico's premier engineering and science university; 12,000+ students; national research reputation
- **Tourism:** Aguadilla beaches, Ríncon surf scene, Cabo Rojo ecotourism
- **Agriculture:** Coffee (interior), plantains, various crops
- **Former military:** Aguadilla's economic history shaped by Ramey Air Force Base (closed 1973); now airport and industrial park
- **Expat community:** Ríncon has significant U.S. expat population (surfers, retirees) — unusual for PR municipalities
- **2020 Earthquakes:** Southwest Puerto Rico (Ponce/Guanica area, just south of this district) was the earthquake epicenter; western PR felt significant effects

---

## Political Profile

- More competitive than Bayamón metro; both PNP and PPD have significant presence
- Mayagüez has historically been competitive
- Western agricultural communities lean PPD in some areas
- San Sebastián: gave birth to PD under Jiménez — interesting local dynamic

---

## Key Issues

1. **Economic development:** Western PR feels economically left behind vs. metro
2. **University support (UPR Mayagüez):** Critical institution; PROMESA cuts affected it
3. **Tourism development:** Aguadilla, Ríncon, Cabo Rojo have tremendous potential
4. **Post-earthquake recovery:** Southwestern municipalities still recovering from 2020
5. **Agricultural support:** Western coffee and crop agriculture
6. **Airport access:** Aguadilla's international airport is underutilized; potential regional hub
