# Senatorial District VIII — Carolina

## Overview
- **District Number:** VIII (Octavo)
- **Region:** Northeastern Puerto Rico
- **Character:** Urban, airport-adjacent, beach tourism, suburban

---

## Geographic Coverage
Distrito VIII covers northeastern PR, likely including:
- **Carolina:** ~158,000; third most populated municipality; Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport is here
- **Canovanas:** ~46,000; semi-urban, suburban of San Juan metro
- **Loíza:** ~26,000; Afro-Puerto Rican cultural capital; festival of Santiago Apóstol
- **Trujillo Alto:** ~75,000; suburban municipality between San Juan and Carolina

---

## Key Characteristics

### Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport
Carolina hosts Puerto Rico's primary international airport — the economic gateway of the island. The senator from Distrito VIII is positioned on all issues related to:
- Air travel and tourism
- Economic development tied to airport
- Hotel and tourism industry (Isla Verde in Carolina is PR's main hotel strip)

### Loíza: Afro-Puerto Rican Heritage
- Loíza is the center of Afro-Puerto Rican culture in Puerto Rico
- The **Festival de Santiago Apóstol** (July) is one of PR's most important cultural events; features African-derived music (bomba, plena), vejigante masks
- Loíza is one of PR's poorest municipalities; faces significant environmental justice issues (industrial facilities near residential areas)
- Historically marginalized; progressive and advocacy organizations are active
- Loíza represents the Afro-Puerto Rican story that is often invisible in mainstream PR politics

### Isla Verde / Carolina Tourism Corridor
- Hotel strip adjacent to airport
- Significant tourism employment
- Beach communities (Ocean Park is technically San Juan but culturally continuous)

---

## Political Profile

- Carolina and Trujillo Alto are competitive
- Loíza's historically marginalized status creates different political dynamics — community organizing is strong
- The airport economic connection gives this district unique federal relations importance

---

## Key Issues

1. **Tourism and airport economy:** The senator here is expected to protect and develop this economic engine
2. **Loíza economic development:** One of PR's most chronically under-resourced municipalities
3. **Afro-Puerto Rican rights and recognition:** Loíza as cultural heritage center
4. **Environmental justice:** Industrial facilities near Loíza communities
5. **Infrastructure:** Roads connecting Carolina to metro; flooding in low-lying areas
6. **Housing:** Gentrification pressure near Isla Verde/Ocean Park corridor
