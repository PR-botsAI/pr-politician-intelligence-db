## Key Facts
- Carolina borders San Juan to the east
- Home to Luis Muñoz Marín International Airport — PR's main airport
- Isla Verde: Tourist/hotel zone
- Roberto Clemente's hometown — strong cultural identity
- Loiza: Historic Afro-Puerto Rican community; cultural significance; historically underserved
- Trujillo Alto: Suburban residential community

## Key Issues
1. Airport corridor development
2. Loiza community services and historic preservation (Afro-PR heritage)
3. Flood zone management (Isla Verde, coastal areas)
4. Public safety (some sectors)
5. Housing and development pressure near airport

## Political Significance
- Loiza has historically voted progressive/PPD; growing MVC support
- Carolina urban area competitive PNP/PPD
- Isla Verde business community PNP-leaning
