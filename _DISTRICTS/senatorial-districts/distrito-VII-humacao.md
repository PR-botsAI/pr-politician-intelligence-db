# Senatorial District VII — Humacao

## Overview
- **District Number:** VII (Séptimo)
- **Region:** Eastern Puerto Rico
- **Character:** Manufacturing, bioluminescent bays, luxury tourism, agriculture

---

## Geographic Coverage
Distrito VII covers eastern PR, likely including:
- **Humacao:** ~52,000; commercial hub of eastern PR; pharmaceutical manufacturing
- **Fajardo:** ~35,000; ferry hub to Vieques and Culebra; Las Croabas fishing community; El Conquistador resort
- **Las Piedras:** ~40,000; inland, diverse
- **Naguabo:** ~22,000; coastal, small manufacturing
- **Yabucoa:** ~34,000; coastal, sugar history, easternmost coast
- **Maunabo:** Small southeastern municipality
- **Vieques:** ~8,000; island-municipality; former U.S. Navy bombing range; ecological paradise/contamination paradox
- **Culebra:** ~1,800; small island-municipality; world-famous beaches

---

## Key Characteristics

### Vieques: The Most Complex Municipality in PR
- **U.S. Navy presence (1941–2003):** The Navy used two-thirds of Vieques as a bombing range for 60 years
- **Health crisis:** Vieques has cancer rates significantly above Puerto Rican averages; attributed by residents and researchers to Navy bombardment contamination
- **The struggle:** Years of protest by Vieques residents, Puerto Rican activists, and international supporters forced the Navy to leave in 2003
- **Current status:** The former Navy lands are being cleaned up by EPA (slowly) as Superfund sites
- **Tourism:** Vieques has some of the Caribbean's most pristine beaches and the famous Mosquito Bay (bioluminescent bay)
- **Political significance:** Vieques is a symbol of resistance in PR — a rallying point for independence and anti-military sentiment

### Pharmaceutical Hub
- Humacao area has pharmaceutical manufacturing — part of the pharma belt that runs through PR's northeast

### Fajardo: Gateway to the Islands
- Ferry terminal connecting to Vieques and Culebra
- Luxury tourism (El Conquistador resort)
- Fishing community (Las Croabas)

---

## Key Issues

1. **Vieques health and cleanup:** Navy contamination cleanup is slow and inadequate
2. **Island municipality access:** Vieques and Culebra depend on ferry system that is often unreliable
3. **Tourism and nature:** Fajardo and Humacao as tourism gateways
4. **Manufacturing:** Maintaining pharmaceutical base
5. **Coastal vulnerability:** Eastern PR is hurricane landfall risk; Humacao area was severely hit by Maria
