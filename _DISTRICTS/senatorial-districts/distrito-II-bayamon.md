## Geographic Overview
District II is the Bayamón suburban corridor — Puerto Rico's largest suburban market directly west of San Juan. Highly developed, commercial, residential sprawl. Suburban middle-class families, commercial strips, industrial zones.

## Key Demographics
- Working and middle class suburban families
- Strong PNP base — one of the most reliable PNP districts
- Older demographic in established neighborhoods
- Younger families in newer developments

## Key Issues
1. **Traffic and roads** — Bayamón is highly car-dependent; traffic is chronic issue
2. **Public safety** — urban crime in some sectors
3. **Economic development** — commercial zones, jobs
4. **School quality** — large student population
5. **Flooding** — some flood-prone areas after heavy rains

## Political Significance
- PNP's most reliable large district
- A PNP senator here has safe seat in most years
- PPD's challenge: break through suburban PNP dominance
